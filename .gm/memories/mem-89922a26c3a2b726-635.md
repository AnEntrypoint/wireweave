gm-method: a class prop passed to a design-system component factory is not guaranteed to be forwarded onto the rendered DOM element -- some factories special-case certain content shapes (e.g. an untitled/first panel auto-detected as a 'Hero' shape) and substitute their own internal markup/classes entirely, silently discarding the caller's children and class overrides. Verify by browser-witnessing the actual live outerHTML/classList of the rendered element, never assume a prop landed just because the local source of the factory (which may differ from the actually-deployed/published version) appears to destructure and forward it.
